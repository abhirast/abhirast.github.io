About me

Welcome to my personal space on the web! I'm Abhinav Rastogi, a passionate technologist, researcher, and educator with a rich blend of technical prowess, managerial competencies, and community service woven through my career. My journey in the tech realm has been marked by a steadfast dedication to pioneering advancements in natural language understanding and dialogue systems, leveraging the potent capabilities of large language models (LLMs) and deep learning to enhance conversations between humans and computers.

Since my early days at Stanford University, where I honed my skills under luminaries like Prof. Andrew Ng and Prof. Christopher Manning, to my impactful tenure at Google, I've always been driven by a quest to push the boundaries of AI to make technology more accessible and useful for everyone. My work focuses on the scalability of task-oriented dialogue systems, where I've led initiatives from conceptualization to deployment, impacting products like Google Assistant, Google Cloud, and various customer support frameworks.

Through my journey, from delving into the possibilities of deep dialogue systems, exploring the expansive potential of LLMs for dialogue, to refining the efficiency of LLMs, I've embraced challenges and celebrated the advancements that we've been able to achieve. It's been a thrilling adventure, one that I continue with great anticipation for what lies ahead.

For collaborations, speaking opportunities, or just to say hello, feel free to reach out. Let's explore together how we can make the interaction between humans and machines more natural, intuitive, and impactful.
